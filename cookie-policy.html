This Cookie Policy explains how Global Travel Deals Platform uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and more useful.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Provide insights into website usage and performance

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Third Party
Session Cookies Maintain your session and preferences during your visit Session only No
Analytics Cookies Track website usage and performance metrics 2 years Google Analytics
Preference Cookies Remember your language and display preferences 1 year No
Marketing Cookies Deliver relevant advertisements and content 90 days Advertising partners

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that we use to enhance your experience:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our website
  • Advertising Networks: Deliver relevant travel advertisements
  • Social Media Platforms: Enable social sharing and integration

Affiliate Tracking

We use cookies to track affiliate link clicks and purchases. This helps us maintain our platform and provide free travel content. These cookies do not collect personal information.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings
  • Cookie Consent: Use our cookie consent banner to manage preferences
  • Third-Party Opt-outs: Opt out of specific third-party cookies
  • Delete Cookies: Remove existing cookies from your device

Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • Personalized content and recommendations
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Some interactive features

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
